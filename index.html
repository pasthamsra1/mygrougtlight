SERVICES

WE Provide Better

Tiling

Tiling involves covering a surface with tiles, which are usually made of materials like ceramic, porcelain, glass, or stone. It's a popular method for floors, walls, and backsplashes due to its durability, ease of cleaning, and aesthetic appeal.

Grouting

Grouting is the process of filling the spaces between tiles to enhance stability, prevent moisture infiltration, and improve the overall appearance of tiled surfaces.

Regrouting

Regrouting involves removing old grout and replacing it with new grout. This process is often necessary when the existing grout has become stained, cracked, or deteriorated.

Epoxy

Refers to a type of grout made from epoxy resins and hardeners. It's known for its durability, moisture resistance, and ability to withstand stains.

silicone

Tiling and construction, typically refers to a type of sealant made from silicone rubber. It is commonly used for various applications, particularly in areas where flexibility and water resistance are important.

Caulking

Caulking is the process of applying a flexible sealant to fill gaps and joints in various construction and home improvement projects. It helps prevent air and water leaks, improves energy efficiency, and enhances the overall appearance of a space.
